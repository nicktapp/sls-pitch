TAPTICO DESIGN SOP v1.0 - KEY GUIDELINES

BRAND COLORS:
- Background: #000000 (Pitch Black)
- Primary Text: #FFFFFF (White)
- Secondary/Muted Text: ~#8b9ab5 (cool grey)
- Accent / Highlight: Navy/Royal Blue (#003087 or similar) + Cyan (#00FFFF) for glow effects
- CTA Button: Navy Blue background, White text
- Navigation Background: dark/semi-transparent

LOGO RULES:
- Dark backgrounds: WHITE logo variant (Whitelogo-nobackground.webp)
- Light backgrounds: BLACK logo variant (Blacklogo-nobackground.webp)
- Logo dynamically switches based on theme

TYPOGRAPHY:
- Headlines: Clean modern sans-serif (Space Grotesk or similar), bold/heavy weight
- Body: Inter or similar clean sans-serif
- H1: Large, cinematic (clamp 2.8rem to 5.5rem)
- H2: clamp 2rem to 3rem
- Body: 1rem, line-height 1.6-1.8
- Min text size on mobile: 16px

LAYOUT:
- Section Padding: 100px top/bottom
- Container Max-Width: 1100px
- Hero: 100vh, full screen
- Section Style: content-driven (not all 100vh)

ANIMATION STANDARDS:
- Scroll-triggered reveals: fade up from opacity 0, translateY 28px
- Word-by-word stagger: 0.05s per word, GSAP or CSS
- Accent Line (Lightsaber Effect): 4px wide, Navy Blue or Cyan, glow blur 12-20px, left edge of section container, opacity 70-90%
- Watermark Numbers: 400px font, 4% opacity, white, left-aligned vertically centered
- CTA Button hover: scale(1.03), increased glow, 0.2s ease
- Mobile fallback: replace complex animations with simple fade-in on scroll

SIGNATURE TAPTICO DESIGN ELEMENTS:
- Accent "lightsaber" line on left edge of premium sections
- Large watermark numbers in background of multi-step sections
- Cinematic full-screen hero
- Glowing effects on interactive elements
- Step-and-repeat logo watermark background (greyscale)

QUALITY CHECKLIST:
- Layout feels cinematic and full-screen
- All text legible with sufficient contrast
- Brand colors applied correctly
- Animations trigger at right points
- CTA stands out clearly
- Mobile layout clean and readable

SLS BRAND:
- Logo: sls_main_logo_full_color.png (sun rays above "SOUTHERN LIGHTING SOURCE" text)
- Colors: Black/dark brown text, gold/yellow sun rays
- Use on dark backgrounds with appropriate treatment (white version or on a contrasting panel)
