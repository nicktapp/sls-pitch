On-Premise Infrastructure

Your AI lives at your office.
Not in someone else's cloud.

For enterprise clients who demand dedicated AI infrastructure, Taptico deploys the NVIDIA DGX Spark — a purpose-built AI computing system that sits at your physical location and runs the full OpenClaw stack locally.

This isn't a shared cloud service. It's your private AI engine, running your agents, processing your data, at your address.

  • All AI processing happens on-site — no sensitive data transmitted to third-party clouds without your explicit permission
  • OpenShell sandboxed runtime isolates agent operations from your host network
  • Privacy Router enforces policy-based guardrails you define during onboarding
  • Hardware is owned by SLS — Taptico's IP is the agent configuration, not the machine
  • Agents continue running locally even if Taptico's infrastructure goes offline

leaves your control.
